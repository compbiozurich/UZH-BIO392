Day II - Gene Disease Relation

Sandrin Hunkeler (MSc. in Informatics)

Variants and diseases

Search Strategy

For ClinVar I filtered for Germline, Pathogenic and Multiple Submitters. Then I selected one of the top most promising variations. For ClinGen I switched between searching for the gene and the disease. The search results for all elements were unambiguous. For the Gene Product and the Disease I searched online using Medi+.
